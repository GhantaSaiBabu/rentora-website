Privacy Policy

January 2026

Rentora respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and mobile phone number
  • Email address (optional, if provided by the user)
  • Location data used only while the app is open to show nearby vehicle hubs
  • Booking details and rental history

2. Location Usage

Rentora accesses location data only when the application is actively in use. We do not collect location data in the background or when the app is closed.

3. Data Sharing

User information is shared only with the respective vehicle owner after a booking is initiated, solely for communication and service fulfillment. Rentora does not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

4. Payments

Rentora does not collect or process payments within the application. All rental payments are handled directly between users and vehicle owners.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Rentora may use trusted third-party services such as Firebase for notifications and Google Maps for location-based features. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Rentora is intended only for individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion at any time by contacting our support team.
