Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Rentora, we value the trust you place in us when sharing your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to being transparent about the data we collect and how we use it to provide a seamless vehicle rental experience.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate local rentals, we collect information that helps us identify you and your location:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Geolocation: Real-time location data to show nearby rental shops (only with your explicit consent).
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system to optimize app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for operational purposes:

  • Connecting you with local vehicle owners for bookings.
  • Processing rental requests and managing delivery/pickup.
  • Sending important service updates and security alerts.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve the Rentora interface.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information is only shared with verified rental shop owners specifically to fulfill your booking request.

4. Your Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we continuously monitor our systems to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to remember your preferences and location settings. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.
