FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rentora?

Rentora is a doorstep delivery vehicle rental platform in India that provides bikes and cars through customer and partner mobile apps.

Are payments done in the app?

No. For transparency, all payments are handled directly between the user and the owner.

Does Rentora own vehicles?

No. Rentora is a platform that connects you with independent local rental owners.
