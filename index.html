What is Rentora?

For Users

Easily find independent rental shops around your location. No hidden middleman fees.

  • Browse bike and car rentals
  • Send requests to owners
  • Direct shop coordination

For Owners

Digitalize your local rental business and reach more nearby riders instantly.

  • List fleet for nearby users
  • Receive booking requests
  • Direct customer contact

Rentora is a technology platform and does not own or maintain any vehicles.

Important Information

  • Rentora does not own, manage, or maintain vehicles.
  • All payments are handled directly between users and owners.
  • Rentora does not collect or process rental payments in-app.
  • Vehicle safety and documents are the owner's responsibility.
