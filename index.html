What is Rentora?

Rentora is a technology platform that connects customers with independent local vehicle rental owners. Rentora does not own, manage, or maintain the vehicles listed on the platform.

How It Works.

01

Discover

Users find nearby rental vehicles listed by independent local shop owners.

02

Request

Users send a booking request. A booking is confirmed only after the owner accepts the request.

03

Use & Return

Users pick up the vehicle, use it responsibly, and return it to the owner in the same condition.

Important Information

  • Rentora does not own or maintain vehicles.
  • Rentora does not collect or process rental payments.
  • All payments happen directly between users and owners.
  • Vehicle safety and documents are the responsibility of the owner.
